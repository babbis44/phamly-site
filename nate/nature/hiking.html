Hiking

Trail hiking is how I spend a lot of my outdoor time. I’m always looking for new trails to explore around Ohio and neighboring states — from short nature walks to longer day hikes that push a bit harder.

Getting Outside

Hiking gives me a chance to disconnect from screens and clear my head. I prefer trails with varied terrain — forests, ridgelines, and waterways — where there’s something to look at beyond the path itself.

Most of my hiking is done in Ohio, though I look for opportunities to get out to new areas whenever travel allows it.
